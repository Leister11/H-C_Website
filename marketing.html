SEO

Search Engine Optimization is one of the most important tools a business can use to gain organic growth online. Creating a website that is optimized for Google and Bing is no simple task, as it involves a host of factors including, but not limited to, researching markets and competitors, keywords, clean code, relevant and well structured content, as well as what other pages link to your site. For law firms, this can be an even more complex task based on the amount of competition in the market. One of my favorite resources for SEO development is Google’s SEO Starter Guide
This website was made with SEO best practices in mind and I am always looking to gain a deeper understanding of SEO concepts and techniques.
